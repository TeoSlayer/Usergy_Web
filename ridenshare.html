Project information

  • Category: Ticketing application
  • Client: Saga festival SRL
  • Project date: 01 December, 2021
  • Project URL: www.beatact.net

Our purpose was to revolutionize the electronic music event stage by creating a centralized platform where Organisers can easily create, promote, sell and manage their events. Customers should be able to find events at the click of a button, without the hassle of searching intensively on social media. Our core idea is to focus on the niche of electronic music events.

We created an easy to use platform with which our users will actively engage. This marketplace is built to create a fully developed ecosystem for organizers and attendees. We integrated modern technologies that will make the process of buying tickets and selling tickets as fast as possible.
