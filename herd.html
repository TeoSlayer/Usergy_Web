Project information

  • Name: Herd
  • Category: Social Response App
  • Client: Herd Network LTD
  • Project Date: 22 September, 2021
  • Project URL: www.herd.network
  • Status: Completed

Herd utilises the power of technology to increase preparedness and engage volunteers in emergency situations. HERD connects people in need to volunteers within their community that can help. When in need, you can send out a ‘flare’ with a short description of what you need help with and an urgency level. Members of your community can then respond to these flares as they come in, facilitating the coordination of volunteer effort to ensure that help gets to those who need it when a crisis emerges.
